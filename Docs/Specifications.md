# Specifications

The software is expecting the constraints and has the dependencies listed in this document.

## Minimum Requirements

The minimum requirements the software must meet:

* 30 FPS
* 2 Player experience on the same keyboard
* Re-bindable keymaps
* Multiple map layouts that may be chosen

## Hardware and Software Expectations

This software is expecting the following out of its user's machine:

* Intel i7 1750h or better
* Google Chrome web browser 143.0.7499.193 or better
* Javascript V8 14.3.127.17 or better
* At least 100 MB of free RAM
* At least 1 GB of free system storage
